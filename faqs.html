Frequently Asked Questions

What is Avishkar?

Avishkar is the flagship event of IEEE - VBIT SB. It is a Technical Paper Presentation competition where students present their ideas and innovative solutions.

When and where will Avishkar 2K25 be held?

Avishkar 2K25 will be conducted on 3rd March, 2025 and 4th March, 2025 at Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology.

What are the tracks in Avishkar 2K25?

The tracks in Avishkar 2K25 include various domains, with each domain featuring problem statements that are organized into specific tracks.

What is a problem statement?

A problem statement is a brief description of the problem or challenge that must be addressed in the idea and presentation.

How can I register for Avishkar 2K25?

Participants can register by scanning the QR code or by filling the registration form available on the official website and Social Media Handle.

What is the last date for registration?

The registration deadline is 28th February, 2025.

Is there any registration fee for Avishkar 2K25?

There is no registration fee for Avishkar 2K25.

What is the mode of participation?

You can participate as an individual or as a team of two.

Can my teammate be from a different branch?

Yes, any first-year student from VBIT of any branch can be your teammate.

Is there a specific format for the Idea and PPT?

Yes, there is a specific format must be followed. Sample templates of Idea and PPT will be sent via email after the registration and are also available on the Social Media Handle.

I don't have a laptop or computer. How can I prepare my Idea and PPT?

Yes, a specified format must be followed. Sample templates of Idea and PPT will be sent via email after the registration and are also available on the Social Media Handle.
